BioSensor: M001A

A biophysical module in healthcare

The Bio-module measures and transfers various types of bio-data including ECG, RESP, GSR, HR, PPG, HCM and BIA etc, integrated Bio-Processor with Analog Front-End to make an all-in-one health monitoring solution.

The algorithm core runs in Bio-Processor,including SPO2,motion recognition,dynamic heart rate monitoring, trend analysis of blood pressure, Emotional and mental stress etc.
